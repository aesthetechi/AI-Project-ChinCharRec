INTRODUCTION
Hello! This is my AI Project, where I trained an AI model to recognize chinese characters. Please keep coming back to see updates and progress!








TO USE:
1) Write the character in the black box below.
Available characters are 零，一，二，三，四，五，六， 七，八，九，十，百，千，万，and 亿. Attempt to draw the character as close to the sample data shown above as the model is sensitive to the way in which the character is written.
2) Press the 'Predict' button to have the model make a prediction on your writing.
3) Scroll down to see predicted character along with probability and the bar chart showing of the probabilities for all of the characters.
